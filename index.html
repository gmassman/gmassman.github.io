About

Posted at — Oct 20, 2019

Welcome to my website! Feel free to poke around and see some of my content and projects.

In early 2017 I moved from Tennessee to San Francisco. Currently, I work as a software engineer at Skupos where we build point-of-sale integrations for convenience stores. I mostly work on the back-end web services, but I have a reasonable amount of front-end experience as well. When I was hired, I helped build our version 1 dashboard in EmberJS. Now, most of my projects involve Ruby or Go, with a healthy dose of MySQL too.

Some of my hobbies include reading, writing, playing and listening to music and learning new technologies. I’m a big fan of non-fiction, especially books that show me a new perspective. I also enjoy fiction and the occasional tv show. Over the past decade I’ve written nearly 100 songs and about 150 poems. Links to some of them are scattered around this site.
