Puddle

Posted at — Jul 26, 2020

Specks of light glinter on the water's surface
 As I close my eyes in meditation
 Breathing deeply, warm air fills my lungs
 Drifting inot the lake's call of calmness

Minutes pass as air goes in and out
 Feeling more aware of life around me
 Fish are swimming, bees and birds are singing
 An old man is whistling on the dock

Separating from the world around me
 I observe the nature of my mind
 Always needing senses to be active
 Now's the time to turn off all the inputs

Focusing on breathing feels so easy
 When momentum keeps me still in tune
 Time slips by, and I've become a puddle
 Pondering beside a lake with sunlight beaming down
