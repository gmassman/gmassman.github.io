While True

Posted at — Oct 20, 2019

I haven't done so and such in a little while
 What's a once in a while that is any more than a little?
 It must be a long while, longer than the littlest little while
 A cardinality all its own, an infinity of sorts
 Discovered once a moment
 Infinitesimally summed across a minute
 Integral to all we've been through
 All we'll be through
 All we'll be

In obsession comes regression
 From the short engaged the long
 Here's a section unimpressioned
 Here's a fraction never wrong

What is written to and from here
 Where it's leading, where it's been
 Which arrangement it's preceding
 Why it's designed just to win
