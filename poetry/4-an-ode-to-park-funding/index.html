An Ode to Park Funding

Posted at — Oct 22, 2019

The ocean is salty
 And ships are at sea
 If the lighthouse were open
 How happy I'd be
 But funding is missing
 We're locked out today
 We can't scale the lighthouse
 Cause Congress won't pay
