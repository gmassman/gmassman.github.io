The Soldier

Posted at — Oct 22, 2019

The soldier follows orders
 He does not question his commander
 His job is well defined - do what you're told
 He has no inherent direction,
 No goals or aspirations
 He is a machine, ready to be programmed
 When his training is complete, he can operate without fear or doubt.
 His goals are not his, they are predetermined.
 His general calls the shots, he simply aims and pulls the trigger.

The soldier is a strong man
 Emotionless to all observers
 Ostensibly aware that each decision is preprogrammed,
 Each outcome is the roll of a die.
 Live or die, he cannot say
 Nor does he have a preference.
 Carrying out orders is his duty
 And putting himself in front of bullets is an order.

His general was once a soldier
 Now he gives the commands.
 He learned long ago that free thought is anti-productive
 For if he were to think freely, his soldiers would have no reason to follow orders.
 The general is still a soldier

Others may question the soldier's actions.
 They'll jeer and point and call him a conformist incapable of abstract thought
 A mindless creature made to march for his entire life
 Never to question why his path is drawn from point A to point B
 But he will get a promotion and maybe become a general one day.

Then the Soldier will understand what it means to lead, because he is the ultimate follower.
