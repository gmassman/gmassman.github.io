Grey Mind

Posted at — Oct 23, 2019

My room with some light
 Cast from many angles
 Where they land and where it goes
 So some becomes more
 Something and something else

Tile and grout
 Wall against will
 Twisting in fractals
 Approaching themselves
 And away from some simultaneously

Smaller and smaller connections
 Closer and closer, denser and denser
 Left and right
 All my neighbors
 So the synapses evolve where quanta resides
 And resolving in rightful places

Sanity breaks down
 Beneath logic, above physics
 Truth is merely a relative resolution of reality
 All we get is truthiness or falseness
 Conscience reveals itself as consciousness
