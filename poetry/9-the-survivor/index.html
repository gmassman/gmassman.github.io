The Survivor

Posted at — Jan 3, 2020

What waves that were rolling and splashing ashore
 What winds that were raging the coastline that day
 What ocean gargantuan, waters that tore
 Their houses to rubble, their homes washed away

The fires that burned in the forests so dry
 The flames in the country continued for weeks
 The damage was dealt and the families cried
 As smoke filled the air over valleys and peaks

My Granny was sickly, she coughed every day
 She lived through the floods and the fires
 Her doctor told her she has cancer today...
 How long can one stay a survivor?
